________________________________________________________________________________
           HOW TO SHARE 'VOLTS' WITH YOUR FRIEND
________________________________________________________________________________

STEP 1: PREPARE THE FILES
-------------------------
You need to send him the folder, but NOT everything. 
The easiest way is to ZIP the "Leads app" folder, but EXCLUDE the big "venv" folder if it exists.

FILES HE NEEDS:
[x] main.py
[x] requirements.txt
[x] run_volts.bat
[x] modules/ (Folder)
[x] assets/ (Folder)
[x] .env (OR .env.template) <-- READ STEP 2

STEP 2: API KEYS (IMPORTANT)
----------------------------
You have two choices:

OPTION A: HE USES YOUR KEYS (Easiest for him, You pay)
- Send him your ".env" file.
- PRO: He just clicks run.
- CON: If he generates 1,000 videos, you get the bill.

OPTION B: HE USES HIS OWN KEYS (Best for you)
- Rename ".env" to ".env.template" before zipping (or just delete .env in his copy).
- Tell him: "Open .env.template, paste your GEMINI_API_KEY, and rename it to .env"

STEP 3: HOW HE INSTALLS IT
--------------------------
1. He unzips the folder on his laptop.
2. He double-clicks "run_volts.bat".
3. THAT'S IT. 

The script will automatically:
- Create a virtual environment.
- Install all libraries.
- Open the app in his browser.
